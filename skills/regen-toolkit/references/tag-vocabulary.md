## Systems Concepts (pick 1-3, only if genuinely relevant)

- **Decentralization** - distributed control, no single point of failure
- **Trust-Networks** - reputation, relationships, social trust mechanisms
- **Resilience** - ability to recover from shocks, redundancy
- **Emergence** - complex patterns arising from simple rules
- **Feedback-Loops** - outputs feeding back as inputs, reinforcing or balancing
- **Commons** - shared resources, collective stewardship
- **Polycentric-Governance** - multiple overlapping decision-making centers
- **Circular-Economy** - waste as resource, closed-loop systems
- **Mutual-Aid** - reciprocal support without hierarchy
- **Subsidiarity** - decisions made at the lowest effective level
- **Fractals** - self-similar patterns at different scales
- **Thresholds** - tipping points, critical mass, phase transitions

**Rule:** Only tag systems concepts that the article genuinely engages with. Don't force-fit.
