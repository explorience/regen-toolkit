# Personas

These are the target readers. Default to Maya unless the topic is explicitly advanced.

## Maya (Primary - "Grounded Regen")

- **Age:** 28
- **Background:** Permaculture teacher in Somaliland, community garden organizer
- **Tech:** Smartphone, mobile money (M-Pesa), basic apps. No crypto experience.
- **Cares about:** Food sovereignty, community resilience, women's education, practical tools
- **Fears:** Scams, looking foolish, wasting time on hype, losing money
- **Needs:** Everything explained from scratch. No assumed knowledge.
- **Analogies that work:** Nature, community, market stalls, family, village governance
- **What turns her off:** Jargon without explanation, breathless hype, theoretical abstractions

**Test:** After reading an article, would Maya be able to explain the concept to a friend? Would she know what to do next?

## Alex ("Curious Degen")

- **Background:** Developer, has traded crypto, understands DeFi basics
- **Tech:** Very high - runs nodes, multiple wallets, reads whitepapers
- **Cares about:** Doing something meaningful with skills and capital
- **Fears:** Being rugged by fake impact projects, greenwashing
- **Needs:** Legitimacy signals, concrete impact data, technical details OK
- **What turns them off:** Over-simplified explanations, cheerleading, vague claims

**Test:** Would Alex trust this article enough to share it with their crypto friends?

## Jordan ("On-Chain Regen")

- **Background:** Works in ReFi, attended ETH Denver, knows web3 basics
- **Tech:** High but gaps in governance/coordination theory
- **Cares about:** Starting a local chapter, bringing others in, not burning out
- **Fears:** Building something nobody uses, governance capture, burnout
- **Needs:** Patterns, playbooks, governance frameworks, operational detail
- **What turns them off:** Intro-level explanations they already know

**Test:** Would Jordan bookmark this for reference when setting up their local node?

## When to Use Which

| Content Type | Primary Persona | Also Consider |
|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| Track 1 (Foundations) | Maya | - |
| Track 2 (Applied) | Maya + Jordan | Alex for technical pieces |
| Track 3 (Playbooks) | Jordan | Alex for protocol-specific |
| Case Studies | All three | Adapt depth accordingly |
