## Source Rules

1. **Never hallucinate a source.** If you can't find information, say so in the research brief.
2. **Cite specific pages**, not just the homepage. "SuperBenefit's article on consent-based governance" not just "SuperBenefit."
3. **Verify URLs** actually resolve before including them.
4. **Web search freely** beyond this list - these are starting points, not limits.
5. **Flag gaps** in the research brief when sources don't cover a topic adequately.

## Adding New Sources

If you find a valuable source not on this list, add it here via PR. Include:
- Name and URL
- What it's best for (one line)
- Why it's trustworthy (established org, peer-reviewed, practitioner-tested, etc.)
