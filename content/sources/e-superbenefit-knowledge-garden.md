# SuperBenefit Knowledge Garden

The SuperBenefit Knowledge Garden is a living wiki of organizational patterns, governance frameworks, and DAO design principles developed through real-world experimentation.

## Overview

SuperBenefit has been deeply engaged in exploring how web3 tools can enable new forms of organization. Their Knowledge Garden captures learnings from their work with various DAOs and impact organizations.

## Key Sections

- **Organizational Patterns**: Reusable patterns for DAO structure
- **Governance Frameworks**: Decision-making and coordination mechanisms
- **Role Definitions**: Templates for organizational roles
- **Process Templates**: Workflows for common DAO operations
- **Case Studies**: Documentation of real implementations

## When to Reference

- Designing organizational structures for local initiatives
- Exploring governance options
- Looking for tested coordination patterns
- Need frameworks for decision-making

## Content Highlights

The Knowledge Garden is particularly strong on the human/organizational side of DAOs, going beyond technical implementation to address how people actually work together.

## Integration Notes

This source is essential for any Regen Toolkit content dealing with organizational design, governance, or coordination. SuperBenefit's patterns have been tested in real organizations.
