# Foundation Getting Started Guide

Foundation's Getting Started guide provides clear, accessible instructions for creators and artists entering web3, with a focus on NFTs and digital art.

## Overview

Foundation is a major NFT platform that has developed extensive documentation for onboarding creators. Their guides are written for artists and creative professionals who may have no prior web3 experience.

## Key Sections

- **Wallet Basics**: Setting up and securing a web3 wallet
- **Understanding NFTs**: What NFTs are and how they work
- **Minting Guide**: Creating your first NFT
- **Marketplace Navigation**: Buying and selling on Foundation
- **Creator Best Practices**: Tips for success as a web3 creator

## When to Reference

- Onboarding artists and creators to web3
- Explaining NFTs to newcomers
- Setting up wallets for the first time
- Understanding creator economics in web3

## Content Highlights

Foundation's guides are written specifically for creative professionals, using language and examples that resonate with artists. They're particularly good at demystifying technical concepts.

## Integration Notes

For Regen Toolkit content targeting creators, artists, or communities with creative components, Foundation's guides provide excellent supplementary material. They're especially useful for the "creative economy" aspects of local regen work.
