# Ethereum Localism Resources

A collection of resources focused on applying Ethereum and web3 principles at the local level, building on the Eth Localism movement.

## Overview

Ethereum Localism Resources provide frameworks, governance tools, and practical templates for communities applying decentralized technologies to local challenges. This complements the Eth Localism Book (Source M) with more hands-on, actionable materials.

## Key Sections

- **Localism Frameworks**: Conceptual models for place-based web3
- **Governance Tools**: Templates and patterns for local governance
- **Practical Templates**: Ready-to-use resources for local initiatives

## When to Reference

- Designing local governance structures using web3
- Looking for practical templates for community organizing
- Exploring the intersection of localism and decentralization
- Building place-based web3 initiatives
