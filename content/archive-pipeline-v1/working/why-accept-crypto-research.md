# Research Brief: Why Accept Crypto Donations?

**Article:** Why accept crypto donations?
**Target:** Nonprofit/org decision makers
**Sources:** A (ReFi DAO), M (Gitcoin), S (Web research)

---

## Key Facts

### 1. Tax Benefits for Donors
- Donors who give crypto are exempt from capital gains tax
- Donors receive federal tax deduction for total value of donation
- This makes crypto donations MORE valuable to donors than equivalent cash donations

### 2. Growing Donor Base
- 14-16% of US adults hold some form of cryptocurrency
- Majority are males ages 25-44 (millennials and Gen Z)
- Crypto donations to Fidelity Charitable DAFs: $13M (2019) → $28M (2020) → $158M (Nov 2021)
- Pineapple Fund gave $55M in Bitcoin to 50 nonprofits (2018)

### 3. Lower Transaction Costs
- Crypto transactions have significantly lower fees than credit card processors
- Credit cards can take 2-3% or more; crypto can be near-zero
- No payment processing middlemen

### 4. Value Appreciation
- Crypto that appreciates means donors can give larger donations than they would in cash
- Donors hold onto appreciated assets longer, then donate at peak value

### 5. Faster, More Transparent
- Transactions are secure, encrypted, and authenticated
- Blockchain provides transparent, auditable transaction records
- Instant or near-instant settlement

### 6. Easier International Giving
- No exchange fees for cross-border donations
- Bypass traditional banking regulatory processes
- Anyone worldwide can donate without bank interference

### 7. Gitcoin Grants Impact (Source M)
- $60M+ funding distributed
- 5M+ unique donations
- 230+ funding rounds
- 3,715 projects funded
- 270k unique supporters
- $28.2B grantee market cap (all-time high)
- Quadratic funding model amplifies small donations

### 8. Risk Mitigation Strategies
- Use reputable platforms (The Giving Block, Endaoment, Givepact, Every.org)
- Instantly convert to fiat to avoid volatility
- Use multi-signature wallets for security
- Establish clear internal policies
