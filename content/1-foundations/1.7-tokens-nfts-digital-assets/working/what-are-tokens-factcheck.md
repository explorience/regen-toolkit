## Detailed Fact-Check

### 1. Token Definition
> "tokens are digital keys built on blockchain technology that unlock access to things like services, ownership, or voting power"

**Source:** BitDegree  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - aligns with BitDegree definition of tokens as "digital assets built on blockchain technology" that "unlock features within a specific digital ecosystem."

---

### 2. Coins vs Tokens Distinction
> "A coin is cash—native to its own system. A token is more like a gift card or loyalty point that lives within someone else's system"

**Source:** Ledger  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - Ledger explains "A crypto asset with a dedicated blockchain is technically a coin, while assets created on a third-party blockchain are called tokens."

---

### 3. Token Representations
> "Crypto tokens can represent digital certificates of ownership for real-world assets like property or artwork, ownership stakes in a project, voting rights within a platform, or access to services within decentralized applications"

**Source:** BitDegree  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - Direct quote from BitDegree research source.

---

### 4. Fungible Token Definition
> "A fungible token is an asset that represents a fixed value and is often used as a means of payment or to access services within an ecosystem. Each token has the same value and is interchangeable 1:1 with the same type of asset"

**Source:** FINST  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - Direct quote from FINST source.

---

### 5. UNI Token Fungibility Example
> "if you lend someone one UNI token (a popular utility token), it doesn't matter which specific UNI token they give back—all UNI tokens are worth the same"

**Source:** Ledger  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - Ledger explains fungibility using this exact example concept.

---

### 6. NFT Definition
> "Each NFT is distinct and can't be exchanged on a one-to-one basis with another NFT. Non-fungible tokens are unique digital assets that represent ownership of a specific item, such as digital art, collectibles, or in-game assets"

**Source:** BitDegree  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - Direct quote from BitDegree source.

---

### 7. NFT Unique Identifier
> "Each NFT has its own unique identifier and characteristics"

**Source:** Reddit  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - Research cites Reddit as source for this claim.

---

### 8. Smart Contracts / Vending Machine Analogy
> "You insert the exact amount of money and press a button for your desired item. The machine automatically verifies your payment and dispenses what you chose—no human cashier needed. Tokens work through smart contracts"

**Source:** BitDegree  
**Status:** ✅ Verified - BitDegree uses exact vending machine analogy: "the vending machine (acting like a smart contract) automatically verifies your payment and dispenses the item based on predefined rules."

**Status:** ✅ Verified - Direct quote from Zoniqx source.
