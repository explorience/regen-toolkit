# Creating, Buying, and Earning Tokens & NFTs

*Your practical guide to getting started with digital assets in web3*

---

## Introduction

Tokens and NFTs give regenerative communities new ways to value and reward ecological work. You can create them, buy them, or earn them through participation.

Just like permaculture—observe before acting, start small, grow gradually.

---

## Creating Tokens & NFTs (Minting)

Minting = creating a new token or NFT on the blockchain. It's like planting a seed: your creation becomes a verifiable, tradable digital asset.

### How to Mint an NFT

1. **Create an account** on OpenSea or Blur
2. **Connect your crypto wallet**
3. **Upload your content** (art, music, teaching materials)
4. **Add details** — name, description, properties
5. **Confirm** the transaction in your wallet

Done. Your creation now exists on the blockchain.

### Creating Fungible Tokens

ERC-20 tokens (like community currencies) can be created by anyone on Ethereum. Need some technical know-how or no-code tools.

*Exciting possibility: a local token that rewards ecological stewardship.*

## Buying Tokens & NFTs

### Where to Buy Crypto

| Method | Best for |
|--------|----------|
| **CEX (Coinbase)** | Beginners buying with regular money |
| **DEX (Uniswap, Velodrome)** | Peer-to-peer trading without middleman |
| **Onramps (MoonPay)** | Buy with debit card, send to wallet |

### How to Buy on a DEX

1. Visit the DEX website
2. Connect your wallet
3. Select what you're swapping from → what you want
4. Approve token permissions
5. Confirm transaction
6. Check your balance (import token address if needed)

**Pro tip:** Start on Layer 2 (Optimism, Arbitrum). Fees = pennies. Perfect for learning.

### Where to Buy NFTs

OpenSea, Blur, Foundation, Rarible — browse, connect wallet, buy. NFT transfers to your wallet.

## Earning Tokens

Web3's best feature: earn tokens for activities that align with your values.

### Staking

Lock tokens to support a network and earn interest (3-16% annually on Ethereum).

**Three ways:**
- **Solo staking:** Run your own node (needs 32 ETH)
- **Staking pools:** Join others (any amount)
- **Liquid staking:** Get a token (like rETH) representing your staked position — holds value while earning

### DeFi Lending

Lend tokens through lending platforms, earn interest. Some stablecoin pools pay far more than traditional banks.

### Governance Tokens

Protocols often reward early users with tokens that give voting rights. Like becoming a member-owner of a cooperative.

### Learning Rewards

Complete educational modules, earn NFTs. Bankless Academy gives collectible badges for completed lessons.

## Security Essentials

### Token Allowances

When using dApps, you grant permission for smart contracts to access your tokens. **Limit allowances** to your immediate trade size — not your full balance.

### Seed Phrase

Your seed phrase = your keys. **Never share it.** Write it on paper, store securely. Never enter on websites.

### Start Small

Begin with small amounts on Layer 2. Treat early web3 transactions as learning experiments.
