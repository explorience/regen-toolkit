# What is ReFi?

*A simple guide to Regenerative Finance for anyone who wants money to make the world better*

---

## Money That Heals

Most finance is extractive — it takes from the earth and communities, concentrates wealth, and treats nature as free collateral. Regenerative Finance (ReFi) flips this. ReFi asks: **what if money could heal instead of harm?**

ReFi is an inclusive, transparent, and accessible alternative to conventional financial systems [1]. It connects financial tools with environmental and social goals, creating economic practices that actually support our world [2].

---

## The Simple Difference

| Traditional Finance | Regenerative Finance |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Takes from communities | Invests in communities |
| Externalizes environmental costs | Prices in ecological impact |
| Concentrates wealth | Distributes value widely |
| Treats nature as infinite | Recognizes nature's inherent worth |

As one source puts it: sustainability means *maintaining* balance, but ReFi means *strengthening* systems so they actually improve over time [3].

---

## How ReFi Works

ReFi uses web3 tools — tokens, blockchain, smart contracts — to:

1. **Value nature** — Put a price on carbon, biodiversity, or clean water
2. **Fund regeneration** — Direct money to projects that heal ecosystems
3. **Create accountability** — Transparent records show where money goes
4. **Build new economies** — Local communities own and govern financial systems

---

## Why It Matters Now

The climate crisis, biodiversity loss, and wealth inequality all connect to how we finance things. ReFi offers a different path — one where your money can:
- Restore damaged forests
- Support local food systems  
- Fund renewable energy
- Build community wealth
