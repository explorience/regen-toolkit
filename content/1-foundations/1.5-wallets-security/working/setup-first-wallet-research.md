## Key Concepts

### What is MetaMask?
- MetaMask is a free digital wallet designed specifically for managing Ether (ETH) and Ethereum-based tokens. [Source S]
- It functions as a bridge between you and the Ethereum network - think of the Ethereum blockchain as a big digital bank that records cryptocurrency transactions and MetaMask as your personal bank account. [Source S]
- It was launched in 2016 by Consensys and is the most used crypto wallet worldwide. [Source P]

### What is a Wallet in Crypto?
- Digital wallets like MetaMask hold your money, but instead of traditional fiat money like dollars or euros, they hold cryptocurrency. [Source S]
- It allows you to buy, send, receive and swap cryptocurrencies and NFTs. [Source S]

### The Seed Phrase (Recovery Phrase)
- When you create a new wallet, MetaMask provides you with a 12-word "seed phrase" (also called "Secret Backup Phrase"). [Source S]
- This phrase is "algorithmically implausible to hack, even with computing power. 100 bitcoin mining rigs couldn't crack it in 1,000 years!" [Source S]
- Without access to your seed phrase, you cannot recover your wallet if you lose your device. [Source S]

### Gas Fees
- Every transaction in MetaMask requires paying a "gas fee" with a layer-1 coin like ETH, which is necessary for processing transactions on the Ethereum network. [Source S]
- Without this coin, you can't send, swap or sell crypto. [Source S]

## Step-by-Step Setup Process

1. **Download MetaMask** - Go to metamask.io/download and select Chrome/Brave browser or iOS/Android app. [Source S]
2. **Click "Get Started"** - Opens the setup wizard. [Source S]
3. **Click "Create a Wallet"** - Select this if it's your first time. [Source S]
4. **Create a password** - This password only works on the specific device you create it on. [Source S]
5. **Write down seed phrase** - MetaMask will show you 12 words. Write them down on paper. [Source S]
6. **Confirm seed phrase** - MetaMask asks you to select the words in order to verify you wrote them down correctly. [Source S]
7. **Configure preferences** - You can click "No Thanks" to opt out of data collection. [Source S]
8. **Access Account 1** - Your receiving address is ready to receive ETH or ERC-20 tokens. [Source S]

## Examples Maya Would Understand

Just like Maya keeps physical money in a leather wallet, MetaMask is a digital wallet that holds digital money (cryptocurrency). [Source S]

### Seed Phrase = Master Key
The 12-word seed phrase is like the master key to your financial kingdom. If you lose it, you lose access to everything - no key, no money. [Source S]

The name "seed phrase" is indicative of how a seed grows a tree. Your seed phrase has a similarly fecund power but for your finances. [Source S]

If you have a lot of crypto, a hardware wallet (like Ledger or Trezor) is like putting your valuable items in a safe - it stores your crypto offline, making it much harder for hackers to steal. [Source S]

## Security Best Practices

From Bankless Academy [Source P]:
1. Store your recovery phrase securely in a safe offline location
2. Never share your private keys or recovery phrase with anyone
3. Create a strong and unique password for your MetaMask wallet
4. Consider using a hardware wallet in tandem with MetaMask for heightened security
5. Enable "phishing detection" in your MetaMask settings
6. Download wallet warning services like Fire to understand your transactions better

From Other Sources [Source S]:
1. Lock MetaMask wallet when not in use
2. Watch out for phishing attacks - scammers may pretend to be MetaMask
3. Use a hardware wallet (Ledger or Trezor) if holding significant crypto
4. Write down seed phrase on two different sheets of paper, keep in separate safe places
5. Consider using more durable material (wood or metal) instead of just paper for long-term storage

Setting up MetaMask is like opening a bank account in the digital world.
