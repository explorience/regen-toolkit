## Key Concepts (Source-Cited)

### 1. Scale of Crypto Crime
- Global cryptocurrency scam losses reached approximately $9.9 billion in 2024 [Chainalysis 2025]
- US victims reported $9.3 billion in losses to FBI IC3 in 2024 [FBI 2024]
- Investment fraud accounts for majority of losses (~$5.8 billion US in 2024) [FBI 2024]

### 2. Common Scam Types

**Phishing Attacks**
- Fake websites mimicking legitimate services (Coinbase, MetaMask)
- Slightly altered URLs (typosquatting)
- Fake customer support on social media
- Browser extensions stealing wallet data
- Emails claiming urgent security issues [Ledger Academy]

**Investment/Rug Pull Scams**
- Guaranteed returns with "zero risk" claims
- Professional-looking websites with fake testimonials
- Teams with unverifiable identities
- Liquidity pulled suddenly [Chainalysis]
- SQUID token 2021 example: $3.3 million stolen

**Romance/"Pig Butchering" Scams**
- Building emotional connections before financial manipulation
- Wrong-number texts, dating apps as entry points
- Fake trading platforms showing fake profits
- Nearly 40% revenue growth in 2024 [Chainalysis]

### 3. Security Best Practices

**Technical Protection**
- Hardware wallets for significant holdings
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Bookmark legitimate sites, verify URLs manually
- Never share seed phrases/private keys
- Regular software updates [Coinbase Help]

**Behavioral Protection**
- Research thoroughly before investing
- Start with small test amounts
- Verify teams through multiple sources
- Don't fall for urgency tactics
- Request video calls in online relationships [TrustWallet]
