# Your First Crypto Wallet: Getting Started with MetaMask

## What Is a Crypto Wallet?

A crypto wallet lets you send and receive digital money directly—no banks, no middlemen. Think of it like Venmo, but decentralized and running on the Ethereum blockchain.

**MetaMask** is the most popular choice. It works as a browser extension and mobile app, letting you interact with Ethereum-based apps, tokens, and NFTs.

---

## Your Seed Phrase: The Most Important Part

When you create a wallet, MetaMask generates 12 random words—this is your **seed phrase** (or "secret recovery phrase").

This is YOUR key. It can:
- Restore your wallet on ANY device
- Access ALL your funds and tokens
- Never be changed or recovered if lost

**Critical rules:**
- ✅ Write it on PAPER (two copies, two locations)
- ✅ Keep it somewhere safe and offline
- ❌ NEVER type it into a computer
- ❌ NEVER screenshot it
- ❌ NEVER share it with anyone—MetaMask will NEVER ask for it

> 🔐 Fun fact: It's mathematically impossible to hack. Even supercomputers couldn't guess it in thousands of years.

### Garden Analogy

Your seed phrase is like genetic code. Lose it, and you lose the plant—permanently. No backup, no recovery.

## Step-by-Step: Set Up MetaMask

### 1. Download It Right
Go to **metamask.io/download** — only the official site. Pick your browser (Chrome or Brave) or get the app from your phone's store.

⚠️ Scammers copy the site exactly. Check the URL twice.

### 2. Create a Wallet
Open MetaMask → click "Get Started" → choose "Create a new wallet"

### 3. Set Your Password
Create a strong password. You'll need it to unlock MetaMask on this device.

### 4. Watch the Video (2 min)
MetaMask shows a quick video about your seed phrase. Watch it.

### 5. Write Down Your Seed Phrase
This is the moment. 12 words will appear.

**DO THIS NOW:**
- Write clearly
- Check each word's spelling
- Keep the exact order
- Make TWO copies
- Store them in DIFFERENT safe places

### 6. Confirm Your Phrase
MetaMask asks you to select the words in order. This proves you wrote them down.

### 7. Skip Data Collection
Click "No Thanks" when asked about anonymous usage data. Optional.

### 8. Done!

You now have a wallet. That long string of numbers/letters = your **wallet address** — like your bank account number. Share it when you want to receive crypto.

## Essential Security Checklist

Do this TODAY:

- [ ] Seed phrase written on paper (two copies)
- [ ] Copies stored in separate safe locations
- [ ] Strong password set
- [ ] Phishing detection enabled (Settings → Security)
- [ ] Bookmarked metamask.io (never click email links)

### Pro Tips

- **Hardware wallet** — If holding >$1,000 in crypto, get a Ledger or Trezor
- **Metal backup** — Stamp your seed phrase onto metal plates (fireproof)
- **Never share** — Real support will never ask for your seed phrase

## What Are Gas Fees?

Every transaction on Ethereum needs a small fee called **gas**, paid in ETH. Like postage—you need a tiny bit of ETH to send anything.

Without ETH in your wallet, you can't send, swap, or trade.
