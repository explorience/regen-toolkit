# Event Follow Up

*How to maintain momentum after your event*

---

## Why Follow Up Matters

The event ends, but relationships continue. What you do after the event determines whether it was worth it.

---

## Immediate Follow Up (24-48 Hours)

### Thank Attendees
Personal thank you to:
- Speakers
- Volunteers
- Key attendees
- New faces

### Share Resources
- Slides
- Recording
- Links mentioned
- Photos

### Social Posts
- Thank the community
- Tag attendees
- Share highlights

---

## Week After

### Follow Up with New Contacts
- Personal messages to new people
- Invite to next event
- Answer questions

### Feedback Collection
- Survey sent
- Ask what worked
- Ask what to improve

### Content Creation
- Write recap
- Create graphics
- Prepare newsletter

---

## Monthly Review

### Metrics
- Attendance
- Engagement
- Feedback scores

### Learnings
- What worked
- What didn't
- What to change

### Next Steps
- Plan next event
- Update process
- Thank team

---

## Best Practices

1. **Personalize** - Don't send templated messages
2. **Be Quick** - Follow up while it's fresh
3. **Be Specific** - Reference specific conversations
4. **Add Value** - Share useful resources

---

## Common Mistakes

1. **No Follow Up** - Event ends, nothing happens
2. **Too Late** - Wait weeks to thank people
3. **Generic Messages** - Templates that don't feel real
4. **Asking Too Much** - Survey fatigue
