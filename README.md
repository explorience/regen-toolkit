# Regen Toolkit

**Web3 for Regens** - A comprehensive open-source curriculum for regenerative practitioners entering the Web3 space.

## What is the Regen Toolkit?

The Regen Toolkit is a structured learning pathway designed to help people working on regenerative projects understand and leverage Web3 technologies. Whether you're running a community garden, leading a local sustainability initiative, or coordinating global climate action, this toolkit will help you harness blockchain, DAOs, and regenerative finance (ReFi) for your mission.

## Target Audiences

The toolkit is designed with three learner personas in mind:

### Grounded Regen
*Experienced regenerative practitioners new to Web3*

You've been doing the work - running community projects, organizing local initiatives, building sustainable systems. Now you want to understand how Web3 tools can amplify your impact. The Foundations track is your starting point.

### Curious Degen
*Web3-native individuals interested in regenerative applications*

You understand crypto, DeFi, and DAOs, but you're looking for ways to apply your skills to projects with real-world positive impact. The Applied track and Playbooks will show you how regenerative practitioners are using these tools.

### On-Chain Regen
*Those bridging both worlds*

You're comfortable with Web3 AND regenerative work. You're here to find specific implementation patterns, contribute your knowledge, and help build the bridge between these communities. Dive into the Playbooks and consider contributing.

## How to Use This Toolkit

1. **Identify your starting point** - Which persona fits you best?
2. **Choose your track** - Start with Foundations if you're new to Web3, or jump to Applied/Playbooks if you have experience
3. **Learn by doing** - Each module includes practical exercises and real-world applications
4. **Connect with community** - Join a local node or online community to practice what you learn
