# Finding Your People

*How to attract the right community members to your local node*

---

## Before You Find Them

You need to know who you're looking for:

### Define Your Ideal Member
- What values do they have?
- What skills do they bring?
- What can they contribute?
- What do they want from community?

### Know Your Non-Ideal
- Who's not a good fit?
- What values don't align?
- What would make someone leave?

---

## Where to Look

### Online Spaces
- **Discord servers** — Web3 communities
- **Twitter/X** — Hashtags, conversations
- **Reddit** — Relevant subreddits
- **LinkedIn** — Professional groups

### Offline Spaces
- **Local meetups** — Blockchain, tech, sustainability
- **Co-working spaces** — Entrepreneurial community
- **Community events** — Local gatherings
- **University groups** — Student organizations

---

## How to Attract Them

### Content Marketing
Share content that shows:
- Your values in action
- What your community does
- The impact you're making

### Community Presence
Be present where your people are:
- Join conversations
- Add value
- Don't just promote

### Events
Host or attend events:
- Your own meetups
- Others' events
- Conferences

### Relationships
One-on-one outreach:
- Warm introductions
- Personal messages
- Genuine interest

---

## The Funnel

### Awareness
People discover you:
- Content
- Events
- Referrals

### Interest
People learn more:
- Website
- Documentation
- Conversation

### Visit
People attend:
- Come to event
- Join online
- Meet members

### Join
People commit:
- Become members
- Contribute
- Stay

---

## What Attracts People

### Authenticity
Real people, real work, real impact

### Belonging
A community that feels like home

### Purpose
Something meaningful to work toward

### Value
Skills, connections, growth
