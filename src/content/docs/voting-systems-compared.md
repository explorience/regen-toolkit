# Voting Systems Compared

*Different voting systems and when to use each*

---

## Simple Majority

### How
More than 50% wins.

### Best For
- Clear two-choice decisions
- Non-controversial votes

### Pros
- Simple
- Clear

### Cons
- Can polarize

---

## Super Majority

### How
More than 66% or 75% wins.

### Best For
- Major changes
- Constitutional decisions

### Pros
- Requires broad consensus
- Protects minority

### Cons
- Can cause stalemate

---

## Quadratic Voting

### How
Cost increases quadratically with votes.

### Best For
- Complex decisions
- Prioritization

### Pros
- Reduces majority tyranny
- Values intensity

### Cons
- Complex to understand

---

## Conviction Voting

### How
Votes accumulate over time.

### Best For
- Ongoing decisions
- Token-weighted systems

### Pros
- Signals intensity
- Time-weighted

### Cons
- Slow
