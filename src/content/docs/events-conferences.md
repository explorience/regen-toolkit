# Web3 Events and Conferences for Local Nodes

*How to use events and conferences to grow your community*

---

## Why Events Matter

Events and conferences are powerful for local nodes:
- Meet potential members
- Learn from others
- Build reputation
- Find partners

This guide covers how to leverage events.

---

## Types of Events

### Local Events

Your own gatherings:
- Meetups
- Workshops
- Socials
- Working sessions

### Regional Events

Area-wide gatherings:
- Regional conferences
- Meetup groups
- Local blockchain events

### Major Events

Large conferences:
- ETHDenver
- Devcon
- Local blockchain summits

---

## Attending Events

### Before
- Set goals (meet 5 people? learn X?)
- Prepare materials (stickers, cards)
- Research attendees

### During
- Take notes
- Meet people
- Collect contacts
- Attend sessions

### After
- Follow up within 48 hours
- Share learnings with community
- Implement ideas

---

## Hosting Events

### Start Local
- Monthly meetups
- Workshop series
- Informal gatherings

### Build Reputation
- Consistent events
- Quality content
- Good venue

### Scale Up
- Quarterly events
- Partner with others
- Consider sponsors
