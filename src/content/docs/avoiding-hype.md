# Avoiding Hype

*How to communicate authentically without resorting to crypto hype*

---

## The Problem with Hype

Crypto is known for:
- Unrealistic promises
- Get-rich-quick narratives
- Exaggerated claims
- FOMO marketing

This hurts credibility. It attracts the wrong people. It damages trust.

---

## Authentic Communication

### Be Honest About Limitations
- What can blockchain actually do?
- What are the challenges?
- What's still experimental?

### Focus on Real Impact
- Actual use cases
- Real communities
- Tangible benefits

### Avoid Gurus
- Don't position yourself as expert
- Share learning, not advice
- Stay humble

---

## What to Avoid

### Promises
- "Guaranteed returns"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Once in a lifetime"

### Fear
- "Don't miss out"
- "Last chance"
- "X will fail"

### Hype Words
- "Moon"
- "To the moon"
- "100x"
- "Game changer"

---

## What Works

### Education
- Explain how things work
- Share resources
- Enable decisions

### Community
- Real relationships
- Honest conversations
- Shared purpose

### Transparency
- Show real progress
- Admit failures
- Share learnings
