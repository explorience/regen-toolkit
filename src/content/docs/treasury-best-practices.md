# Treasury Best Practices

*How to manage community funds responsibly and transparently*

---

## Why Treasury Matters

Your treasury is your community's resource. How you manage it affects:
- Trust
- Sustainability
- Ability to act

---

## Core Principles

### Transparency
- Everyone can see funds
- Regular reporting
- Clear records

### Accountability
- Rules for spending
- Approval processes
- Audit trails

### Sustainability
- Don't spend it all
- Plan for the future
- Diversify sources

---

## Spending Rules

### Thresholds
- Small (<$100): Coordinator discretion
- Medium ($100-1000): Two signatures
- Large (>1000): Community vote

### Documentation
- Purpose
- Recipient
- Amount
- Approval

---

## Security

### Multi-sig
Multiple people needed to move funds.

### Wallets
- Hardware wallets for large amounts
- Clear backup procedures

### Access
- Limit who has keys
- Rotate access periodically

---

## Reporting

### Monthly
- Income/expenses
- Balance
- Notable transactions

### Quarterly
- Full audit
- Budget review
- Strategy alignment
